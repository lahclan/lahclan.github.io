I have recently been thinking about how I want to live my life and how I can become the best version of myself. Every time I come back to this topic I always stop on the idea of leaving everything (Instagram, iMessage, social circles), only keeping contact with my mom, in order to be able to fully immerse myself in physics or math or whatever subject or thing it is that I want to pursue. Obviously this is kind of silly and unrealistic, but I think it has some merit.

Instagram for instance is something that is just generally bad and only propogates things like narcissism, mental illness, and self-esteem issues. Ridding yourself of it is easier said than done however. A few months ago I switched to a secret Instagram account that I would use just to be able to keep contact with some of my closest friends. At the time I thought it was a pretty smart idea because I would pretty much be able to sever all my ties with people that I didn't really care about and start fresh. I realized that it was actually a pretty dumb idea when people started asking me for my Instagram and I didn't really have any other option but to give it to them. 

I kept trying to come up with loopholes to heavily limit my Instagram account without deleting it like only viewing messages through Meta Business Suite or trying to block DMs or posts from all my followers but I now know that the only true solution is to delete everything permanently and never look back. Doing this will, I believe, isolate me more, but is that really a bad thing? Having more time to focus on the things I actually enjoy can only ever be a good thing.

For the rest of the school year my plan is to only really engage in a few different forms of entertainment so that I can maintain focus for longer and find more enjoyment in things I find interesting. My only real rule for this 'challenge' is that I will only use books, writing, music, and friends for entertainment: this means no Instagram, YouTube, or movies in my free time. My thought process is that, if I find myself bored with these things, I will just study because what else am I going to do while I'm alone in my room.

Thanks for listening to my writing I plan to do more random thought pieces like this in the future. (:
